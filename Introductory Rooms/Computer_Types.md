## Sophia's Summer of Hidden Computers - Month 1

<img width="590" height="284" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/32f7ca97-6743-4830-bb82-680fd1196e15" />

A laptop is a portable, battery-powered computer designed for everyday tasks like emails and documents, but its performance may slow down during long or heavy usage due to limited cooling.

A desktop is a stationary computer that uses a direct power supply, offers better cooling, and provides consistent performance for longer and more demanding tasks.

A workstation is a high-performance computer built for professional work, focusing on accuracy and reliability, and is capable of handling complex tasks like simulations and 3D modeling.

A server is a powerful computer designed to run continuously and handle multiple users at once, often without a direct interface, and it supports networks, websites, and applications.
